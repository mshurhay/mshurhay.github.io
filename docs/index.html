ETHICS/VALUES

    The ethics statement that stands out to me is serving the best interests of the people. This is tenant number four from the International City/County Management Association (ICMA) It is important to represent the individuals over personal interests.

    My values for local government management include:

  • Accountability- always be responsible to other staff members and residents
  • Transparent- always be apparent in the organization even if the results are not positive to the organization
  • Collaborative- engage and work with staff, residents, and businesses to improve the quality of life for the organization
